Aerodynamic Design
of a Solar Car

During my third year of engineering I joined the Flowmeter Laboratory at UTN Santa Fe and began working on the aerodynamic design of a solar-powered vehicle to compete in the Atacama Solar Challenge in Chile. Using Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), we developed and optimised the car's external shape to minimise drag and improve stability under racing conditions, applying ANSYS CFX and mesh generation techniques learned in the laboratory.
