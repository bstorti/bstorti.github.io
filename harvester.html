Pneumatic Olive Harvester:
Design & Construction

This project involved the complete design and construction of a novel type of olive harvester that uses pulses of compressed air to detach olives from the branches without causing any physical damage to the trees. I worked as lead designer and engineer over several years, covering aerodynamic and mechanical design, structural calculations, FEM analysis, and hands-on prototyping. The machine significantly reduces harvesting time compared to traditional manual methods while protecting the tree canopy.
